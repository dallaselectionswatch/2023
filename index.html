Why is this important?

It's important to care about where a campaign gets its money from in an election because it can affect what the candidate cares about and who they are loyal to. When donations come from outside the election district, it can cause problems by making it so that people who aren't even part of the election have too much power to influence it.

Does campaign funding significantly affect the outcome of an election?
