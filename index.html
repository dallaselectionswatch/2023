How to Use the Map

1. Select your district from the dropdown menu.
2. Pick a candidate from the dropdown menu
3. Check out the colors and height! The darker the blue and the higher the tower, the more money that area donated to the candidate's campaign.

Not sure which distict you live in? Click the link below and type in your address to find out!
Find My District

How to Navigate the Map

View Contributions: Click/Hover over a zipcode
Change Viewing Angle: Drag 2 fingers up/down.
Move the map: Drag 1 finger in any direction.
Rotate the Map: Place two fingers on the map and drag up with one finger and down with the other.
